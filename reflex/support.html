FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an account to use Reflex?

No. There's no sign-in of any kind — open the app and start training immediately. Everything is tied to your device, not an account.

Where are my scores and stats stored?

Entirely on your device, using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is uploaded anywhere — see the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset my progress?

Delete and reinstall the app. Since all data lives locally, uninstalling permanently clears every session, streak, and personal best — there's no cloud copy to restore from.

Does Balloon Risk use real odds I can learn?

No — the pop chance for each colour is randomly re-shuffled and jittered at the start of every game, so it can't be memorised across sessions. Results only ever reveal the ordering (safest to riskiest) for that game, never the exact numbers.

Is Reflex free? Are there subscriptions or in-app purchases?

Reflex is free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads of any kind.

What devices and iOS versions are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 26 or later.
