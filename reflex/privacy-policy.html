Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026·Last updated July 22, 2026

Reflex: Quant Prep is designed and developed by Siddharth Rout, an independent developer (referred to here as “we,” “us,” or “the developer”). This policy explains how the app handles your information.

In short: Reflex collects no personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. The app makes no network connections of any kind, so nothing you do inside it ever leaves your device.

1.Information we collect

Reflex does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. The app contains no networking code — it has never made a network request and has no way to do so — which means there is nothing to send, and no server on the other end to receive it.

On the App Store, this corresponds to a “Data Not Collected” declaration: the app gathers no data that is linked to your identity or used to track you.

2.Information stored on your device

Your scores, streaks, and personal bests are saved locally using Apple’s SwiftData framework, in a database that lives only inside the app’s own sandboxed storage on your iPhone or iPad. This information is created and used solely to show you your own progress within the app.

  • This data never leaves your device and is not visible to the developer or to anyone else.
  • If you have iCloud device backups enabled in iOS Settings, your local Reflex data may be included in that backup, exactly like any other app’s. This is Apple’s system-level backup of your own device, encrypted and controlled by you — it is not something Reflex or its developer can access.

3.How we use information

Because Reflex collects no personal data, there is no personal information for us to use, analyze, profile, sell, or share. Your on-device gameplay data is used only by the app itself, on your device, to display your statistics and history back to you.

4.Third parties and data sharing

Reflex does not share any data with third parties, because it holds no data to share. The app is built entirely on Apple’s own frameworks (SwiftUI and SwiftData) and includes:

  • No third-party SDKs or libraries — nothing from an outside vendor runs inside the app.
  • No analytics or tracking — no usage measurement, no advertising identifiers, no cross-app or cross-site tracking.
  • No advertising — Reflex shows no ads and works with no ad networks.

5.Device permissions

Reflex never requests access to your location, contacts, camera, photos, microphone, or health data, and it does not send push notifications. The app requests no privacy-sensitive permissions because it has no use for them.

6.Data retention and deletion

Since no data is collected or stored off your device, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our end. The only Reflex data that exists is the local database on your device, and you remain in full control of it.

Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all of its locally stored data. There is no separate copy held anywhere else, because none was ever made.

7.Children’s privacy

Reflex does not knowingly collect information from anyone of any age, because it does not collect information at all. As no personal data is gathered, transmitted, or stored off the device, there is nothing that could be collected from a child under 13 — consistent with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and comparable regulations elsewhere.

8.Your rights

Privacy laws such as the GDPR and CCPA give you rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Reflex holds no personal data about you on any server, so there is nothing for us to retrieve, amend, or erase. You can exercise complete control over the app’s on-device data at any time by deleting the app.

9.Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of Reflex introduces a feature that requires network access — this page will be updated first, with a new effective date shown above, before that change ships. Material changes will be reflected here so you can review them.
