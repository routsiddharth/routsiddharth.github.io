About VIP

Velaris Investment Partners (VIP) is an intercollegiate, student-run investment fund that combines fundamental and systematic strategies to manage a $60,000 portfolio across 8 top U.S. universities.

With 300+ active members, VIP annually attracts nearly 2,000 interested students nationwide, providing hands-on experience in quantitative investing and algorithmic trading.

Who We Are

VIP is a student-run organization focused on value investing and algorithmic trading, providing members with real-world experience in portfolio management and quantitative analysis.

Investment Strategy

Our investment approach combines fundamental analysis with systematic strategies, utilizing both bottom-up value investing and algorithmic trading to optimize portfolio performance.

Committee

Our national committee oversees the strategic direction of VIP, ensuring consistent quality across all chapters and maintaining relationships with sponsors and industry partners.

Members

Our members are driven students passionate about finance and investing, working together to achieve superior returns through rigorous analysis and innovative strategies.
